FAQ

How do I convert PNG to JPG without uploading it?

Drop the PNG above, choose JPG, and download. The conversion happens in your browser's canvas engine — the file never leaves your device. Transparent areas become white in JPG, which has no transparency.

Should I use JPG, PNG, or WebP?

JPG is best for photos and social uploads, PNG for graphics needing transparency or crisp text, and WebP for the web where you want the smallest file at the same quality.

Can I convert HEIC photos here?

Browsers can't decode HEIC natively yet, so iPhone HEIC files aren't supported. Set your iPhone camera to 'Most Compatible' to shoot JPG, or export from Photos as JPG first.
